GISAID data acknowledgement

ViruSurf_GISAID is enabled by hCoV-19 genome sequence data and metadata from the GISAID EpiCoV™ database. The GISAID organisation promotes the rapid sharing of data whilst protecting the acknowledgement rights of data providers.

If you intend to publish research on or otherwise use sequence data or metadata from GISAID, or the derived data available from ViruSurf_GISAID, you must accept the GISAID Database Access Agreement and appropriately credit (e.g. by attaching as supplementary data) the data contributors. In academic publications you should also cite the GISAID project itself. Details of how to give appropriate credit are available on the GISAID website.
